Path 7.5 of Moon

This was a path that was directly east from the large cavern previous. It is straight, and narrow, and seems not to be horribly long. In a alcove there's a statue of Sindariel, and in her mouth is a crystal bird. When you touch it, faint images of a man holding a helmet drift past you, and a soft voice whispers: "Draw the moth symbol on the right arm." The voice fades, and the moth symbol appears in your mind for just a moment before disappearing.
