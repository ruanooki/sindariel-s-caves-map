Cavern 11 of Bone

After following the path that was decorated with an archway of bones, you find that it ends in a medium sized cavern. The cavern is mostly empty, except for a large black door. There is also a small stone on the ground with a symbol marked on it, along with Y8. Perhaps this Symbol Stone Y8 will be of use.

Large black door: The door opens with an easy push, and reveals polished black bricks in a square room. It appears this door connects Sindariel's caves to Streiya's Labyrinth.
