Sindariel's Caves

As the cemetery was cared for and completed, something strange happened to the mausoleum decorated with the relief of Sindariel's carved face. The metal doors glowed with a soft light, then opened, revealing the twisted caves within. They seem quite expansive, do you dare see what secrets they hold?

OOC: A section of the mausoleum can be revealed once per day (for each member) with the !explore death command! This is not required at all, but can be a fun way to earn birds, and learn a little about a moment in Raehn's history. The areas revealed may be used as rp settings in the cemetery rp channel and the 1 on 1 category rp channels if so desired. Occasionally when exploring an object may stand out. In these situations you may use the !search <item name> command to look more closely at it.
